About

Eethal Pazhagu Trust was established on September 4, 2022, in the vibrant community of Rajest Nagar, Sholinghur, Ranipet. Our trust was founded with a vision to empower underserved populations through comprehensive educational programs, social initiatives, and community engagement. Recognizing the pressing needs within our community, we are committed to creating opportunities that foster personal and collective growth.At Eethal Pazhagu Trust, we believe that education is a powerful tool for transformation. Our programs are designed to provide access to quality education for children and adults alike, ensuring that everyone has the chance to learn and thrive. We offer scholarships, tutoring sessions, and vocational training workshops aimed at equipping individuals with the skills necessary to succeed in todayâ€™s competitive environment. In addition to education, our trust focuses on promoting health and well-being within the community. We organize health camps, awareness programs, and wellness workshops to address critical health issues and encourage healthy lifestyles. By partnering with local health professionals, we strive to provide essential services and information that empower individuals to take charge of their health.
